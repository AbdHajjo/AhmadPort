Good Communication at the Workplace (Restaurant)

Title: "Teamwork in Action"

Panel 1: Setting: A restaurant kitchen.
Manager: "Alright team, remember, clear communication is key to a smooth service tonight!"
Waiter: "Got it! We'll stay on top of orders and keep the kitchen updated."
Panel 2: Setting: The waiter approaches the chef with a clipboard.
Waiter: "Here's the order for Table 5, two vegetarian pastas, one with extra mushrooms, and one side of garlic bread."
Chef: "Perfect, I’ll get that going right away!"
Panel 3: Setting: The dining area.
Waiter serves the food to the table.
Customer: "Wow, everything came just as we ordered. Thanks for the great service!"
Manager: "Great job, everyone, communication is the secret to success!"

Poor Communication at the Workplace (Restaurant)

Title: "Miscommunication Mayhem"

Panel 1: Setting: A restaurant kitchen.
Manager: "We’re short on time, just get the orders out quick, no need to check in!"
Waiter: "Alright... but that could be risky..."
Panel 2: Setting: The waiter approaches the chef with an order pad.
Waiter: "I think it’s two pastas and a side salad, or was it garlic bread?!"
Chef (confused): "I’ll just guess!"
Panel 3: Setting: The dining area.
Waiter serves the wrong orders to the customers.
Customer (frustrated): "This isn’t what we asked for!"
Manager overhears and mutters, "I should’ve paid more attention..."
